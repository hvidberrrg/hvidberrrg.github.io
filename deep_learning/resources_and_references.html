On this page you'll find a collection of resources and references on deep learning and neural networks. We'll include both links to online resources as well as references to books and articles.

Online resources

In this section you'll find links to online resources like massive open online courses (MOOCs) and videos.

Neural Networks for Machine Learning
An interesting and informative MOOC offered by Coursera, created by the University of Toronto and taught by professor Geoffrey E. Hinton. Be prepared to put some effort in especially the programming assignments.
